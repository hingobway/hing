CAPTAIN MIKI AND THE BOIZ

1INT. SMALL ROOM - DAY1

MIKI sits in chair at his computer. He is working in a music program, composing his piece of music. He hums as he works.

MIKI

(to himself)

Aaaand that should do it. My masterpiece is finally complete.

MIKI presses play, and the track PLAYS. He listens through headphones and seems deeply moved and into it. The audience does not hear.

MIKI (CONT.)

Now I just need a totally radical band to perform this sick song.

2TRANS. MAP WORLD2

As a dumb cartoon version of MIKI travels across the screen, possibly across a map of some sort, a narrator speaks...

NARR. (V.O.)

And so the valiant Captain Miki set off on his quest to find himself a band. His first stop, Sean the Slayer!

FADE TO:
3INT. KITCHEN/EATING SPACE - DAY3

SEAN is contentedly sitting in a seat at the table, eating some food (it doesn’t matter what, but some relation to soda is preferable.) Suddenly, MIKI bursts through the door, to the surprise of Sean.

SEAN

What the hell...

MIKI

(exclaiming)

Sean, my dude! I need your help!

SEAN

How did you even get in here?

MIKI

I wrote a song.

SEAN

(quite confused and slightly annoyed)

Um... what?

MIKI

Yeah, well I wrote a song, but I can’t really do it myself so... Wanna join my band?

SEAN crosses his arms.

SEAN

Well, you know, I’m a real pro. I only play the best of the best music.

SEAN looks up, thinking. We see him thinking of some particularly bad concert piece. He makes a face.

SEAN (CONT.)

I think there’s only one way that you can really prove your music to me.

MIKI

(nervously)

Well... what is it?

SEAN acts like he’s going to give a big challenge.

SEAN

You must... fetch me a soda!

MIKI

Uh... ok.

MIKI goes and grabs a soda from the fridge, then gives it to SEAN.

MIKI (CONT.)

Here you go. So will you be in my band?

SEAN

(apathetically)

Eh, sure.

4TRANS. MAP WORLD4

A small SEAN appears at the bottom, as if he’s been “captured/beaten”. (Sean may follow Miki instead.) MIKI continues along screen.

NARR. (V.O.)

Excited, Miki and his new bandmate went in search of more people to fill up their band. Then, Miki found his target — a lil’ boi named Nathan sitting on a bench.

5EXT. PICNIC TABLE, BACKYARD - DAY5

NATHAN is reading a book outside. He has an empty cup with a straw next to him. He picks it up and tries to drink from it. Nothing comes out so he sucks harder and harder, making loud noises and squeezing his face more and more. Camera shakes more and more as his face gets sucked in. Finally MIKI enters.

MIKI

Hey Nathan!

NATHAN whirls around, his face still extremely squished and angry. He tries to speak but cannot open his mouth.

MIKI

AH IT’S A MONSTER! KILL IT!

NATHAN takes the straw out of his mouth.

NATHAN

WHAT WHERE?

MIKI

Oh nice Nathan you killed it. Anyway, I wrote a song and could really use some of your skills.

NATHAN

Captain, I would by no means consider you my friend, but I would consider your music to be my friend. I am very lonely. All you need to do to get me to join you is get me a soda…

MIKI

Oh that’s easy I already did tha—

NATHAN

From the basement.

CUT TO:

A spooky stairway down to a basement. MIKI and NATHAN look down into it.

MIKI

I have to go down there?

NATHAN

It is the only way.

MIKI goes down the stairs and it gets very dark. We hear FUMBLING and then a match is lit.

CUT TO DAYLIGHT

NATHAN

Why are you lighting a match you idiot it’s the middle of the day.

MIKI

Oh yeah sorry.

MIKI opens the door and enters the basement. We hear TRIPPING and CURSING and a few CRASHES. Miki is clearly having trouble navigating the dark basement. Finally he emerges, proudly showing the can of soda he has retrieved.

NATHAN

Oh no I don’t like root beer, that’s for my friend. Could you get me a Coke? They’re right next to the root beer.

MIKI sighs and walks back in with more CRASHING and CURSING. He emerges once more. He holds up the coke.

MIKI

This what you want?

NATHAN

Hell yeah boi!

Nathan turns and looks into the camera for this last line. Mandatory devil horns to increase the effect.

6TRANS. MAP WORLD6

NARR. (V.O.)

Miki, feeling confident, continues on his journey. Little does he know that his next trial will be extremely difficult.

CUT TO:
7INT. LIVING ROOM - DAY7

MIKI walks down the stairs and sees a figure in a chair facing away from him and towards a fire in a fireplace. Miki slowly approaches, eventually reaching the figure. He reaches out and taps the figure on the opposite shoulder. The figure whirls away from Miki to where he was tapped.

DECLAN

WHAT BRINGS YOU HERE PUNY- what? Where did you go?

He looks around, then sees MIKI.

DECLAN

WHAT BRINGS YOU HERE?

MIKI

Wanna join my band?

DECLAN

SURE

8TRANS. MAP WORLD8

NARR. (V.O.)

Huh, I guess I was wrong about it being hard. Anyway,now our boi Miki needs only a few more boiz to join his boi band. One such bo— I’m not going to keep talking like this. Look all you need to know is that he’s gonna do some more dumb stuff with some guy and I am not getting paid enough for this.

CUT TO:
9INT. BEDROOM - DAY9

MICHAEL is in his room, quietly doing some kind of work. A guitar is clearly seen behind him. Suddenly, MIKI bursts out of his closet.

MICHAEL

AUGH! HOW LONG HAVE YOU BEEN IN THERE?!

MIKI

What are you talking about? I just got here. You can be so dumb sometimes.

MIKI pats MICHAEL on the head. Michael is still somewhat in shock.

MIKI (CONT.)

It’s okay. Everyone makes mistakes.

MICHAEL

Whatever. Why are you here?

MIKI

Wanna join my band?

MICHAEL

Oh sure. I have a little homework but I can come with my guitar in like 20 minutes.

MIKI

Oh okay, you want me to do your homework. Don’t you worry, Captain Miki’s got it.

MICHAEL

No no I just need—

MICHAEL is cut off by MIKI putting on glasses dramatically and starting a montage. Miki is seen scribbling on a whiteboard and typing and writing and thinking and coming up with ideas etc.

MIKI

Done.

MIKI shows MICHAEL what he has written. The paper is covered by a large question mark. Michael sighs and takes the paper.

MICHAEL

Just give me 20 minutes and I’ll be there.

MIKI

Yeah that seems like the best solution.

MIKI stands up and slowly backs into the closet and closes the door. The slower the better, and the more staring during this moment, even better.

9.5TRANS. MAP WORLD9.5

NARR. (V.O.)

And so, the mighty Captain Miki continued his quest. This time, he decided to go for two people with one stone.

NARRATOR is confused. Possible PAPER RUFFLING, or MUMBLING in background.

NARR. (V.O.) (CONT.)

Two people with one stone? That doesn’t seem right, now does it? I mean he’s not killing these assholes is he? (beat) Is he?

There is an awkward silence, and then we...

CUT TO:

10INT. ROOM WITH TABLE - DAY10

DUNCAN and FINNEGAN sit at a table, conversing.

DUNCAN

And then she was all like “Dodge this!” and licked his ear!

FINNEGAN

No no she shot him in the head after she—

MIKI (O.S.)

CALLING ALL BOIIS! Calling all bois! Calling al—

MIKI (CONT.)

OH, HEY BOIS. WANNA JOIN MY SIK BAND?

DUNCAN

Um...

FINNEGAN

...why are you here?

MIKI

Well it’s kinda a long story. You see, I was sneaking around in your house, just like I do most days, just, y’know, smelling Duncan’s underwear, reading Finnegan’s diary, when I realized you guys were here too! So I thought I’d stop by.

DUNCAN and FINNEGAN look at each other, confused and slightly amused.

DUNCAN

(giggling)

You have a diary?

FINNEGAN

(giggling more)

You wear underpants?

MIKI

Hey cool! So now that we’re all bonded, wanna consider my offer?

DUNCAN

Well, I don’t really think that we can commit to some crazy band without making sure it’s worth our time.

MIKI

Oh, great. This shit again. What the hell is it you want this time. A manicure? Some pizza? More soda??

FINNEGAN

Ooh a pizza would—

DUNCAN stands up and places himself in a dramatic pose.

DUNCAN

Great Captain Miki, I challenge you... to a duel!

CUT TO BLACK, THEN TO:
11EXT. FIELD - DAY11

MIKI and DUNCAN are standing several feet away from each other in a field, with swords sheathed. Duncan UNSHEATHES his sword.

DUNCAN

Now, may the best man win!

MIKI UNSHEATHES his sword. It is very wobbly and dumb.

MIKI

Looks like I’m going to win then.

DUNCAN

You smug piece of—

DUNCAN has been stabbed in the chest by MIKI. He looks down at the sword, up to Miki, and back to the sword.

DUNCAN

Shit.

MIKI pulls out the sword as DUNCAN topples to the ground. He wipes off the blade on DUNCAN'S clothes.

MIKI

So, will you join my band?

FINNEGAN

Oh yeah sure Duncan will, but you have to do something to prove yourself to me.

MIKI

(sighing)

Wasn’t killing your brother enough to make you like me?

FINNEGAN

In order for me to join your band, you must bring Duncan back to life.

MIKI

Oh, ok.

There’s a brief moment of silence. MIKI glances down at the dead DUNCAN, and we cut to follow his eyes. It then cuts back to Miki. Everyone stays silent, and there’s no sign of magic or anything, just waiting. It then cuts back to Duncan, and instead of lying dead, he is just standing there, and immediately without pause begins his line.

DUNCAN

What? Why am I back here? I must go back! WHAT HAVE YOU DONE?!

DUNCAN grabs MIKI’S shoulders and starts shaking him wildly, shouting and cursing. The camera cuts in a few jerky movements to rapidly zoom out to a final wide shot.

12TRANS. MAP WORLD12

Cartoons of MIKI, SEAN, NATHAN, DECLAN, FINNEGAN, and DUNCAN (who is crying) move out of the field. They start to move back towards the performance venue on the map.

NARR. (V.O.)

And with that, MIKI had assembled his band. So now it was time to move on to stage two— time to perform!

One, two, one, two, three, four!

MICHAEL

Hey guys, sorry I’m a tad late, I just had a lot of wo—
